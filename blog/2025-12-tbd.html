Research Papers Discussed in Class

I have been trying to incorporate research into teaching, [link to wilson nwit paper]. Below is a list of the SE research papers and reports incorporated into my undergraduate-level SE course this semester.

  • Paul L. Li, Amy J. Ko and Jiamin Zhu. "What Makes a Great Software Engineer?". International Conference on Software Engineering (ICSE), 2015.
  • James Ryseff, Brandon De Bruhl, Sydne J. Newberry. "The Root Cause of Failure for Artificial Intelligence Projects and How They Can Succeed: Avoiding the Anti-Patterns of AI". Rand, 2024.
  • Jules White, Sam Hays, Quchen Fu, Jesse Spencer-Smith, Douglas C. Schmidt. "ChatGPT Prompt Patterns for Improving Code Quality, Refactoring, Requirements Elicitation, and Software Design". In: Generative AI for Effective Software Development. Springer, 2024.

There are probably some missing, and does not include other article and blog posts. If you have suggestions for other research that would be useful to include, please share any suggestions!
